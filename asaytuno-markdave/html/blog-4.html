The Dust Beneath the Keys

Mark Dave Asaytuno • Feb 25, 2026 • 3 min read

My keyboard keys were starting to feel sticky and the dust was visible under the backlight. It was distracting while I was trying to code, so I decided to do a deep clean.

Procedure:

  • Took a photo of the keyboard layout for reference.
  • Removed all keycaps using a puller.
  • Used compressed air to blow out the crumbs and thick dust.
  • Cleaned the plate using a small brush and isopropyl alcohol.
  • Soaked the keycaps in soapy water and dried them.
  • Reassembled the keys.

The keyboard feels much smoother now and looks brand new. It was a simple task but it made my setup much cleaner.
